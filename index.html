Here's a time line of Ram Dass life:

  • 1931 born in Maui-Hawai
  • 1960 personally and professionally associated with Timothy Leary at Harvard University.
  • 1962 he conducted research with Leary on the therapeutic effects of psychedelic drugs
  • 1967 Alpert traveled to India and became a disciple of Hindu guru Neem Karoli Baba who gave him the name Ram Dass, meaning "Servant of God"
